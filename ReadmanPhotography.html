About

Readman Photography is a single person freelance operation capturing high quality wildlife and nature photography. I use a Canon EOS 1DX, often rocking some form of telephoto lens, I primarily shoot in the RAW format and edit in a variety of industry standard software. I am based in Redcar and often travel around Teeside to capture stunning shots of wildlife and the nature around me. I sell my photos for use in Projects, Advertising, Television, Media and a wide variety of places across industries.
